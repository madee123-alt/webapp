Tasks to earn badges

  • 7-Day Streak: Mark at least one habit complete every day for 7 consecutive days.
  • 30-Day Streak: Maintain any habit for 30 consecutive days.
  • 100-Day Streak: Maintain long-term consistency for 100 days.
  • Perfect Week: Complete at least one habit each day for 7 days.
  • Early Bird: Complete a habit before 8 AM at least once.
  • Night Owl: Complete a habit after 10 PM at least once.
  • 10/50/100 Completions: Reach the total number of task completions across all habits.
